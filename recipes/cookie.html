Cracked Cookie Recipe

Description

Our curated collection of cracked cookie recipes invites you to experience the perfect blend of crispy exteriors and gooey, chewy centers.Our easy-to-follow instructions ensure that both seasoned bakers and kitchen novices can create these delectable treats with confidence.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 packet vanilla
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Take sugar into a deep bowl.
  2. Add cocoa on top.
  3. Pour in the vegetable oil and mix well.
  4. Add two eggs and continue to beat.
  5. Sift in the flour, vanilla, and baking powder.
  6. Knead until you have a homogeneous mixture. It will be sticky in consistency. Do not add extra flour for it to harden. Let it rest in the refrigerator for 25 minutes.
  7. In a separate bowl, add powdered sugar. Using a spoon or ice cream scoop, take round portions of the dough and coat them in powdered sugar.
  8. Place the dough balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  9. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius, without the fan, on the middle shelf, until they crack, for approximately 15 minutes. Enjoy your meal!
